# Generation Differences

This document summarizes key mechanical differences between generations implemented in the engine.

## Gen 9 (Scarlet/Violet)
- **Terastallization**: Changes user's defensive type, adapts STAB (1.5x -> 2.0x if matches original type).
- **Hail -> Snow**: Hail damage removed, Snow boosts Ice-type Defense by 1.5x.
- **Recovery Moves**: PP reduced to 5 (max 8).
- **Paradox Abilities**: Protosynthesis/Quark Drive.

## Gen 8 (Sword/Shield)
- **Dynamax**: Doubles HP, access to Max Moves.
- **Speed Updates**: Speed changes apply immediately mid-turn (Dynamic Speed).
- **Terrain**: Boost reduced from 1.5x to ~1.3x (5325/4096).

## Gen 7 (Sun/Moon)
- **Z-Moves**: One-time nuke moves requiring Z-Crystal.
- **Burn**: Damage reduced from 1/8 to 1/16.
- **Dark Void**: Accuracy reduced, only Darkrai can use it.
- **Prankster**: Dark types immune to Prankster status moves.
- **Paralysis**: Speed drop changed from 0.25x to 0.5x.

## Gen 6 (X/Y)
- **Mega Evolution**: +100 BST, ability change, type change.
- **Fairy Type**: Introduced.
- **Crit Damage**: Reduced from 2.0x to 1.5x.
- **Weather**: Permanent weather abilities (Drought, etc.) nerfed to 5 turns.
- **Steel**: Lost resistance to Ghost and Dark.

## Gen 5 (Black/White)
- **Gems**: One-time 1.5x boost to corresponding type move.
- **Weather Wars**: Permanent weather was dominant.
- **Team Preview**: Introduced (Standard).

## Gen 4 (Diamond/Pearl)
- **Physical/Special Split**: Moves categorized individually, not by type.
- **Choice Scarf**: Introduced.
- **Stealth Rock**: Introduced.

## Gen 3 (Ruby/Sapphire)
- **Abilities**: Introduced.
- **Double Battles**: Introduced.
- **EV System**: Modern 510 cap system replaced Stat Exp.

## Gen 2 (Gold/Silver)
- **Held Items**: Introduced.
- **Special Split**: Special split into Sp. Atk and Sp. Def.
- **Types**: Dark and Steel introduced.
- **Weather**: Rain Dance, Sunny Day, Sandstorm introduced.

## Gen 1 (Red/Blue)
- **Special Stat**: Combined Special Attack and Special Defense.
- **Crit Chance**: Based on base speed.
- **Freeze**: Permanent (no random thaw chance, only Haze/Fire moves).
- **Hyper Beam**: No recharge if it KOs.
- **Binding Moves**: Prevent target from moving.
